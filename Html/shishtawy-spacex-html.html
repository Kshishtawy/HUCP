From a dream to colonize Mars, SpaceX was born

What is SpaceX anyway?

SpaceX was founded in 2002 by Elon Musk with the goal of reducing space transportation costs to enable the colonization of Mars.

(Sounds like some text copied from wikipedia... lets just say it's an amazing super-cool rocket company, ok?)

Time to Give Up?

After the third failure in row and the Millions of dollars wasted, giving up seemed like a reasonable choice. However, Elon was determined enough for the fourth attempt,
the attempt that changed the course of history.

"I don't ever give up. I'd have to be dead or completely incapacitated."

- Elon Musk

SpaceX Makes History

Elon was trying to achieve reusable rockets, which can fly to space and come back in one piece. This revolutionary idea came to life with the fourth attempt. Soon after that, NASA made a $1.6 Billion business contract with SpaceX.
