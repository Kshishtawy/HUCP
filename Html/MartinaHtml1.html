introduction to medicine

Medicine ( Latin : ars medicina ), meaning the art of healing; It is the science that gathers human experiences in caring for the human being, and the sicknesses , diseases and injuries that affect his body, psyche , or the environment in which he lives . Medicine also deals with the conditions that encourage the occurrence of diseases and ways to avoid and prevent them. One of the aspects of this science is attention to health conditions and conditions, and an attempt to improve them. Medicine is an applied and practical science that benefits from human experiences throughout history. In the modern era, medicine is based on scientific studies documented by laboratory, clinical and anatomical experiments.
