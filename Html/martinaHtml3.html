Achievements of Dr.Magdi Yacoub

The seventh day presents the most important achievements of Dr. Magdi Yacoub, which history does not forget: 1: Established the first center in Aswan to treat children with heart diseases, entitled Magdi Yacoub Foundation for Heart Diseases. 2: Magdy Yacoub contributed to saving the lives of many children in the world who were born with a congenital defect in the large blood vessels. 3: He underwent an open heart operation for the English actor Eric Morekemp, and he also succeeded in performing another operation for the international actor Omar Sharif. 4: He has a major role in developing cardiovascular surgery programs in the Arabian Gulf, Mozambique and Ethiopia regions. 5: Foundations of heart transplant surgery in Britain. 6: He established a charitable association entitled Chains of Hope with the aim of helping children born with congenital heart defects, and finding modern ways to save their lives.

Facts about Magdi Yacoub

He has performed more than 20,000 heart surgeries in Britain. A British team has succeeded in developing a heart valve based on stem cells. Serves as an ambassador for organ transfers. The Petchey Academy west of London is named after him. Established a free-of-charge cardiology center in Aswan

Dr. Magdi Yacoub visits Egypt every period during which he performs many open-heart surgeries for free. Dr. Magdi Yacoub established a center for heart operations in the city of Aswan in Upper Egypt in 2009. A statue of him was established in Aswan and another in Belbeis in a square bearing his name in appreciation of his great role in the field of medicine
